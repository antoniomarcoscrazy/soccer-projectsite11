Become a Manager

  • Managers are hired by the club’s shareholders.

  • Manage your team, set tactics and scenarios.

  • Buy and Sell players on the transfer market.

  • Earn weekly wages from the club.

  • Make sure your shareholders are happy to avoid getting sacked.

Club Shareholders

  • Shareholders own the clubs.

  • Trade club shares on the decentralised marketplace.

  • Receive dividends from your club’s income (if not in debt).

  • Vote in or fire the managers.

Become a Player Agent

  • Agents are hired by player shareholders.

  • An agent can put pressure on a club by controlling the player’s morale, which affects performance.

  • Agents receive a percentage of the soccer player’s wages.

  • Agents act as a spokesperson for the shareholders and communicate directly with their manager and other managers regarding moves and contract lengths.

Player Shareholders

  • Shareholders own the players.

  • Trade player shares on the decentralised marketplace.

  • Receive commissions from your player’s wages.

  • Receive bonuses if a player is played, scores or assists.

  • Vote in an agent to represent the player.
