Line Up

​
Line Up is where managers decide who is on the pitch to start, who the substitutes are, and who the extra players that won’t be participating in the match are. It shows:
  • Which players are on the pitch and their positions (11)
  • Which players are available for substitutions (7)
  • Which players aren’t participating in the match (the rest of the club’s players, between 3 and 14)
To change the line up, drag and drop a player onto another player. They’ll swap positions. You can submit the line up by clicking the Submit Team Sheet button.
