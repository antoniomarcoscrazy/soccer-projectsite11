Tactics

Tactics is where managers set their strategies for matches. After making any changes (including situations), you must click/tap the Submit Team Sheet button or your changes will be lost.
Tactics MUST be submitted at least 12 hours BEFORE the next match. If there are less than 12 hours before the next match, you cannot change your tactics.
NOTE ON DATE & TIME: The date format is <day of the week> <day number> <month> <2-digit year>, e.g. Tues 6 Jun 06 in simple ISO date format is 2006-06-06. Time is given in UTC+0 time, so you must adjust for your timezone. Club managers should consult their Tactics page to see the counter for when they must submit their team sheets by.
