Agents Determine Transfer Availability

​
Whether or not a player can be transferred depends on the player’s agent. By default, players are available for transfer. However, an agent can change this so that the player cannot be transferred. For an agent to change the transfer availability of a player, the agent must:
  1. 1.
    Visit their player’s screen
  2. 2.
    Click the CONTRACT tab
  3. 3.
    Beside Available for transfer, check either Yes or No
  4. 4.
    Click the Submit button
    ​
NOTE: If an agent’s player is listed as available for transfer, and a club manager then lists the player in the transfer market, the agent can’t reverse the listing by changing the player’s availability to No after the fact. At that point, it’s too late.
