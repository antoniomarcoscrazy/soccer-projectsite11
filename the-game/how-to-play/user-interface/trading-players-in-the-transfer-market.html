Trading Players in the Transfer Market

​
The transfer market is an auction where players are traded between clubs. Agents determine if a player can be traded while managers buy/sell players on behalf of their club.
There are 3 tabs in the Transfer Market:
  1. 1.
    Transfer List: This is where players are listed for auction
  2. 2.
    Transfer History: Records of all past transfers
  3. 3.
    Free Bench: Bidding on a player here begins the 7-day auction in the Transfer List
