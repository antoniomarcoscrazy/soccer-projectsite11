A Quick Note on the Beta and Support

Should you encounter any problems, your first troubleshooting step should be to reload the page. On desktops, press CTRL + F5. On mobiles, in the MetaMask web browser, tap the ellipsis button (3 dots) in the lower-right corner then tap the Reload button. You can also try signing out and then signing in again.
