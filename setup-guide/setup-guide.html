Setup Guide

Beta IV
The following are required to play Soccerverse:
  1. 1.
    The MetaMask / Coinbase and maybe other web3 wallets with Polygon compatibility (a browser based cryptocurrency wallet)
  2. 2.
    Your web3 wallet configured for the Polygon blockchain
  3. 3.
    Some MATIC and WCHI (aka CHI) cryptocurrency
The procedures for desktop browser clients and mobile device clients are similar with only a few minor differences. For the sake of simplicity and clarity, this guide will go over what you need to do on desktops and mobile devices separately.
The following guides will guide you through the Metamask wallet setups however you may wish to use another wallet such as the "Coinbase Mobile Wallet".
NOTE: If you are having trouble following this guide, please join our Discord server for help and support.
