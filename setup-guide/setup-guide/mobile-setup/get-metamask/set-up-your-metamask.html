Set Up Your MetaMask

Once installed, you must properly configure MetaMask to use it. The following will guide you through the process step-by-step to create a new wallet.
  1. 1.
    On the Welcome to MetaMask screen, tap the Get Started button
    ​
  2. 2.
    On the Wallet setup screen, tap the Create a new wallet button
    ​
  3. 3.
    On the next screen, tap the I Agree button
    ​
  4. 4.
    You must protect your wallet with a password. Enter one that you WILL NOT FORGET… AND RECORD IT SAFELY!
    ​
  5. 5.
    You can optionally toggle the Remember me switch on
  6. 6.
    Check the “I understand that MetaMask cannot recover this password for me.” checkbox
  7. 7.
    Tap the Create password button
  8. 8.
    Your wallet has a secret recovery phrase that you must NEVER share with anyone. Click the Start button to begin getting your SECRET RECOVERY PHRASE.
    ​
  9. 9.
    Read the Secure your wallet screen to understand the importance of your secret recovery phrase
    ​
  10. 10.
    Click the Start button to continue
  11. 11.
    Make certain that nobody is watching over your shoulder, then tap the View button to reveal your 12 secret recovery phrase words.
    ​
  12. 12.
    Write them down on PAPER and store them in a safe place. If you lose this you will not be able to recover your wallet and all your funds in your wallet and in Soccerverse will be PERMANENTLY lost.
  13. 13.
    On the Confirm Secret Recovery Phrase screen, tap each of the words in order
    ​
  14. 14.
    Tap the Complete Backup button to continue
  15. 15.
    CONGRATULATIONS! YOU’VE SET UP METAMASK PERFECTLY! Tap the Done button
    ​
  16. 16.
    You’re now ready to add Polygon and get some MATIC and WCHI
    ​
