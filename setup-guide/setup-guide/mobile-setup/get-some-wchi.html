Get Some WCHI

To get some WCHI, you can swap some of the MATIC you purchased for WCHI (or CHI).
  1. 1.
    On your MetaMask main screen, tap the Swap button
  2. 2.
    On the Swap screen, tap the Select a token drop down menu
  3. 3.
    In the Convert to field, type CHI or WCHI
  4. 4.
    In the results list, tap the XAYA CHI result
  5. 5.
    Tap the Continue button
  6. 6.
    Enter an amount of MATIC to spend on WCHI
  7. 7.
    Tap the I understand button
  8. 8.
    Confirm your decision by swiping the Swipe to swap bar
  9. 9.
    Wait a few moments and your WCHI will arrive in your MetaMask wallet
  10. 10.
    Go and play Soccerverse!
