Get Some MATIC

There are far too many different ways to get MATIC, so only 1 way is described below. For help with other ways, search online or ask in our Discord server.
The simplest way to get MATIC is through the MetaMask wallet using a credit/debit card. To do that:
  1. 1.
    On the main screen, tap the Buy icon in the middle of the screen
    ​
  2. 2.
    Read the “What to Expect” screen then tap the Get started button
    ​
  3. 3.
    On the Buy Crypto Tokens screen, select your region, i.e. country, etc., then tap Continue
    ​
  4. 4.
    You may have more than 1 payment method available to you on the Payment Method screen, depending on your region. Choose one if you have the option. Tap the Continue to account button.
    ​
  5. 5.
    On the Amount to Buy screen, make sure that MATIC is selected, and enter the amount that you wish to spend in your local currency, e.g. GBP, CAD, USD, EUR, KRW, CNY, etc., then tap the Done button and then tap the Get quotes button
    ​
  6. 6.
    Quotes are dynamic and change very quickly. If you wait too long, you’ll need to tap the Get new quotes button
    ​
  7. 7.
    Tap the “Buy with XXXX” button
    ​
  8. 8.
    Follow the instructions as you would with any regular online purchase. Your MATIC will be sent to you after you’ve completed the transaction
You may need to wait a few minutes. Just be patient. Your MATIC will be sent to your wallet. Usually this is very quick.
Next, you can use some MATIC to buy WCHI. Just make sure you keep some MATIC for blockchain transaction fees.
