Get Some MATIC

MATIC is the cryptocurrency of the Polygon blockchain. It is required for performing actions in Soccerverse. This is to prevent spam on the network and is generally extremely small in actual $ costs.
You can purchase MATIC on various exchanges, however, the easiest and quickest way is within the MetaMask wallet itself. Once you purchase MATIC you can then easily purchase WCHI using the MATIC.
To purchase MATIC in MetaMask:
  1. 1.
    Open MetaMask by clicking on the browser extension (you may need to click this button to pin the extension in your browser so that you can see it)
  2. 2.
    Click the Buy button
  3. 3.
    Purchase some MATIC by following the instructions on the screen. Later you will convert some of this to WCHI, so buy as much as you think you want to spend in Soccerverse. Once completed, the MATIC will appear in your wallet. The minimum you can purchase by this method ranges from around $5 to about $20 or about £16. This should be more than enough to get started in Soccerverse.
