Get Some WCHI (aka CHI)

WCHI (or just CHI), is the cryptocurrency of Xaya. Xaya is the underlying technology that Soccerverse runs on. WCHI is used to create accounts and for in-game purchases.
  1. 1.
    To get some WCHI, click the Swap button in MetaMask
  2. 2.
    Enter the amount of MATIC you want to convert to WCHI.
  3. 3.
    In the Swap to field, type CHI and select CHI (XAYA)
  4. 4.
    Click Continue if it pops up a message
  5. 5.
    Click Review Swap
  6. 6.
    Click Swap
You should now have everything you need to start playing. You will see your MATIC and your WCHI (as CHI) in the assets section of your MetaMask wallet
You can now create an account and play Soccerverse on the Soccerverse website.
For help or questions, visit our Discord server and ask in an appropriate channel.
