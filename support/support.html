Support

This guide and other information is available at the Soccerverse website:
​https://soccerverse.pro​
Most information is available in this guide, i.e.:
​https://docs.soccerverse.pro​
For further support, visit our Discord server here:
​https://discord.gg/ze5xJgg7AM​
There are different channels in there, so choose the most relevant for your questions or comments. Some of the most relevant channels are:
  • #support: Get help about how to play the game here
  • #bug-reports: If you find a bug, check to see if it’s in here, and if not, post it here with as much information as possible
  • #new_releases: Soccerverse is updated regularly, and you can find information about that here
  • #manager-job-offers-and-trades: If you want to become a club manager, this is a good place to start
  • #soccerverse-notifications: See below!
